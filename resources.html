LaTeX Resources. All problem set answers must be typeset in LaTeX. LaTeX is the most widely used tool to professionally typeset mathematical content, proofs, conference and journal papers, etc. It is installed on all CS lab machines (both OS X and Unix) and is freely available for all major operating systems, as well as in browser-based platforms such as Overleaf. If you have not had experience with LaTeX in CSCI 256 or other courses, you may find the following resources helpful:

  • Learn LaTeX in 30 minutes
  • Doing your HW in LaTex

Note that you are allowed use online resources or AI tools to help debug your LaTeX code or typeset your figures in LaTeX.
